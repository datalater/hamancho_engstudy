### Korean-Disassemble-JHI

A: 아, 이런. 내가 무슨 생각을 한거야?
  왜 내가 내 성을 적어 넣은거지?

   이제 티켓이 네 이름으로 되어 있네. 100유로만 내면 돼.

  왜 내가 적어 넣은거지, 내 성을?

  티켓이, 되어 있네, 네 이름으로, 이제. 내면 돼, 100유로만.

A: Oh my god. What do I think like that?  
  Why do I write down my last name?    
B: So, Did you get the new ticket?  
A: Not yet. here it is. I just got the agreement.  
   The ticket is in your name now. Now you only pay 100 euros.


+ 내 성을 적어넣다: put in(enter) my last name.
+ 새 티켓을 받다: get a new ticket.
+ 확약서를 받다: got a confirmation.



### Correct

A: Oh no. What was I thinking?  
   Why did I put in my last name?  
B: So, did you get a new ticket?    
A: No, not yet. It's here.I just got a confirmation.
   The ticket is in your name now. We just have to pay 100 euros.


> **Note**:
+ 다행이다: What a relief. I relieved.  
+ 내가 어떻게 이럴 수 있어 : I can't believe I did this.
+ 해결해줬다: They fixed it.
