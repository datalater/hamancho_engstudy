# EBS Easy English 20190412

## Dialog

### Source

* **A** 아르만, 이거 12부 복사해줄래?
* **B** 그래. 걱정 마. 이거 회의 때 쓸거야?
* **A** 응. 회의까지 15분 남았네.
* **B** 잘됐다. 너한테 할 말이 있어. 나 식당을 차릴까 생각 중이야.
* **A** 뭐라고? 정말이야? 한 번 더 생각해보는 게 어때?
* **B** 난 거의 마음을 정했어.

### Draft

* **A** Arman, could you copy this 12 papers?
* **B** Okay. Don't worry. Is this for a meeting?
* **A** Yes. I have 15 minutes to the meeting.
* **B** That's great. I have something to tell you. I'm thinking about running a restaurant.
* **A** What? For real? Why don't you think one more time?
* **B** I almost made up my mind.

### Answer

* **A** Arman, can you please make twelve copies of this?
* **B** Okay. Don't worry. Is this for the meeting?
* **A** Yes. Well we have 15 minutes before the meeting.
* **B** Nice. I have something to tell you. I'm thinking of running my own restaurant.
* **A** What? Are you serious? Why don't you give it a second thought?
* **B** I've almost made up my mind.
