A: Who wanna eat pancake?     
B: Me. Smell is really nice, mom.     
C: I don't wanna eat. I don't have an appetite.     
A: Then, I'm going to make soup.      
C: Thank you, mom.     
A: Do you think you can go to the school today?    
C: Yes, I feel better now.          


+ 죽을 끓여줄게: I'll make some porridge for you.
+ 오늘 학교에 갈 수 있을 것 같니: Do you think you can go to school today.



### Correct

A: Who wants pancakes?   
B: Me. They smell so good, mom.   
C: I'll pass. I don't have an appetite.   
A: Oh, then I'll make some porridge for you.    
C: Thank you, mom.    
A: Do you think you can go to school today?  
C: Yepp. I'm feeling better now.     


> **Note**:
+ 저는 괜찮습니다: Not me. I'm OK. I'll pass.
+ 학교에 갈 수 있겠니? : Are you able to go to school?
